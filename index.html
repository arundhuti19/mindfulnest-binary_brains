Our Mission

To provide accessible, compassionate, and effective mental health support to individuals in need. We aim to break down barriers to care, reduce stigma, and empower individuals to lead healthier, more fulfilling lives. Our platform offers a range of resources, an AI chatbot for personal assistance, and educational materials designed to meet the diverse needs of our users. We strive to create a safe and supportive community where everyone can find the help they need to thrive. By leveraging technology and human connection, we are committed to making a positive impact on mental health worldwide.

About Us

We are Arundhuti Sinha and Mahika Das, first-year BCA students at Techno India, deeply passionate about mental health and wellness. Our mission is to create a supportive community that provides accessible mental health resources, raises awareness, and helps individuals lead healthier, happier lives.
